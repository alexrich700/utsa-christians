Christian Students
on Campus

Christian Students on Campus is a registered student organization at the University of Texas at San Antonio. We have been joyfully serving the UTSA campus and San Antonio community for over 16 years. We love the Lord Jesus and His word, aspire to know the truths of the Bible, experience the riches of Christ, and share the gospel with those around us. Come grow with us!
