Our Stories

Marco

Marco

Hello, my name is Marco. I'm a junior at UTSA majoring in Art.
Entering college the Lord told me to find friends who share the same faith as I do. I agreed with the Lord and made a goal for myself: to find a christian club, because that would be the best way to connect with other christians. By the grace of God, I found this group during my orientation. Ever since then, I quickly found my place in this club. Seeing that I was able to meet all brothers and sisters in Christ who come from all different backgrounds made me ecstatic. The fact that that this group took really good care for me warmed my heart and revealed to me that this is what Christianity is all about: the caring, the fellowship, the praying, just to name a few!
I recommend anyone who is open to Christian fellowship to join Christians on Campus at UTSA. They won't regret it! I thank the Lord for finding this club and the memories that I have made with them so far!

Nana

Nana

My name is Nana Alli and I'm an alumni at UTSA. Prior to meeting Christians on Campus at UTSA I always had questions concerning the Lord Jesus and the Christian faith. On my first day of classes, I saw a big "Christ is Life" banner hanging on a table at the club fair in the Student Union, and couldn't help but approach them. That was the best decision I made in my life.

During my time in college, Christians on Campus was my gateway to a genuine Christian life. The bible studies ignited and gradually strengthened my faith in the word and the Lord. And the club connected me to a community of believers whose living impressed wad encouraged me to love the Lord more. Even after graduation, i'm still reaping the benefits of being with the group. Praise the Lord!

Rafiel

My name is Rafael Porras and I am a recent graduate of UTSA. I received the Lord Jesus into my heart during my senior year in high school school and found the meaning of my human life. I made the decision to keep pursuing God while in college. Upon arriving at UTSA I connected with Christians on campus and found a group that helped me grow in my daily christian walk.

Attending bible studies and getting together with other students to talk about Jesus strengthend my faith and encouraged me to speak to others about God. Through this time in college I gained many experiences with God that will keep me pursuing.

Jose

Jose

Hi my name is Jose Gamboa and I’m an alumnus from UTSA. My major was electrical engineering and graduated in 2012.
I grew up in Peru going to some church meetings every now and then, pretty much only when my uncle would take me. When I moved to San Antonio with my family, we went to church meetings more regularly but then we got busy with life and forgot all about it. In my high school years, I felt like I was the only believer among my classmates since I was the only one that would talked about God here and there. So I kind of felt alone. Sometimes I read the Bible and also I would get inspired to talk to God but I was never consistent. However, I did get help from those times and got closer to God. Then as my high school years were coming to a close, there were some tragedies of some family friends and at the end of it all my grandma’s death happened right on my graduation night. These situations led me to questioned life and to wonder what the purpose of my life was.
When I came to UTSA, something came to my heart: I need other believers to help me to pursue the Lord and survive as a Christian. Then, I met Christians on Campus at UTSA as a freshman in my second semester. I began to read the Word with brothers and because of that my love for the Lord increased as well. In this group I realized that God doesn’t want me to do anything for Him except to give myself to love Him. I remained with the club till I graduated and in during those years I came to learn what the purpose of my life was. God’s purpose is that He wants us to love Him and be filled with Him! When I learned God’s purpose that automatically answered my purpose as well.
Song of Songs 1:4 Draw me; we will run after You
I'm now a member of the staff of Christians on Campus at UTSA. I look forward to running together with students who love the Lord!
